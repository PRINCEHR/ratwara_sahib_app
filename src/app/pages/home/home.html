About us!

VGRMC Trust was founded by Sant Waryam Singh Ji inspired by his mentor Sant Isher Singh Ji of Rara Sahib. The Samparada is linked with Bhai Sahib Bhai Daya Singh Ji, leading Piara (Beloved) of the Five, selected by Guru Gobind Singh Ji. Sant Maharaj Ji founded Gurmat Parchar Centre, Gurudwara Isher Parkash at Ratwara Sahib in 1986 for spreading Gurmat (Sikh Teachings) in India and abroad, started publishing monthly magazine 'ATAM MARG' in 1995, published books and brought out audio-videos.
